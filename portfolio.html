Artworks

Below are some artworks that I am quite proud of!

This is a sketch I did of a traditional Chinese style building. I lived in China for 8 years before moving to Canada (after living there for 3 years I came here to Killeen, Texas), so I have always admired certain parts of the Chinese culture.

These are some clay sculptures that I made last year for my art class. They are from the movie 'My Neighbor Totoro',which is a classic Japanese anime movie. I loved the movie as a kid (and still do now),so I decided to make some clay Totoros in memory of the movie. To make the sculpture, I used air-dry clay to form the shapes, waited for the clay to completely dry, and then painted the colors using acrylic paint.

This one isn't exacty an artwork but it holds special meaning to me. I joined the American Red Cross back in 2020 and I participated in the Young Ambassador Program. Before Christmas in 2020, we made thank-you cards to active soldiers living in our area. Out of dozens that I made, this one is my favorite!
